Why Myanmar?

On the ground floor of the illegal drug trade are struggling farmers without a choice. While many would prefer not to grow opium or methamphetamine, their livelihoods depend on it. That’s why our Morning Rocket team set our sights on Myanmar, one of the world’s largest hubs for these illicit drugs.

By sourcing coffee from single origin sources and microlots in Myanmar, we empower these farmers to make a living without going against their values.

The challenge that we are striving to overcome is with Australian roasteries that seek out premium coffee at the lowest possible price. While Myanmar is superior to their mass market competitors, they do not have enough production volume to lower the cost. In turn, Morning Rocket has taken a direct-to-consumer approach where ethical sourcing comes first.

Order today if you’re ready to take a sustainable sip that gives these farmers a choice!
